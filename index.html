How It Works

Our proprietary agentic AI framework handles the complete PCB design process from text input to Gerber files.

1

Natural Language Processing

Our NLP engine parses technical specifications from plain text descriptions, extracting requirements, constraints, and components needed for the design.

2

Component Selection & Sourcing

Specialized agents match requirements to optimal components from our database of over 1 million parts, considering availability, cost, and performance characteristics.

3

Automated Circuit Design

Circuit topology is generated using our proprietary algorithms that account for signal integrity, thermal management, and design for manufacturing principles.

4

PCB Layout & Routing

Layer-specific agents collaborate to place components and route traces, ensuring optimal layouts while meeting electrical and mechanical requirements.

5

Production-Ready Output

Complete Gerber files, BOM (Bill of Materials), and assembly instructions are generated and validated for immediate manufacturing submission.
